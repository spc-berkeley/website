About Us

SPC (Software Products @Cal) is the first student chapter of the International Software Product Management Association (ISPMA). Under the guidance of ISPMA Board Member Haragopal Mangipudi, our chapter launched in January 2024.

We are a non-profit, student-run consulting organization providing data-driven, actionable solutions to clients using techniques taught by industry leaders.

OUR MISSION

Empowering Product Leaders

"At ISPMA, our mission is to empower individuals to become skilled product managers by fostering a dynamic, innovative community at UC Berkeley. Through education, hands-on experiences, and a focus on excellence, we equip members with the tools to navigate and lead in the evolving field of product management. Our goal is to shape visionary leaders who drive positive change in the technology industry."

WHY CHOOSE SPC?

Complete Experience Beyond Academics

Join a vibrant community that combines rigorous product management education with meaningful social connections. From industry networking and hands-on client projects to exclusive retreats and team bonding experiences, we create lifelong friendships while building your PM career. As the first university chapter with direct ISPMA connection, you'll gain access to unique professional opportunities and an unforgettable college experience.
